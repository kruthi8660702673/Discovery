Founded in 1994 by Mike Meldman, Discovery land company is

a US-Based real estate developer and operator of private residencial

club comunities and resorts witha world-renewed portfolio of

domestic and international properties.

Our Mission

We don't build buildings, but rather we build setting

Where families can create intergeration memories

and enrich their lives in exquisite and untouched

sanctuaries. everything is driven by the

fundamental desire to create spaces where families can

be together.


These settings offer a relaxed and comfortable sense of

comunity featuring outstanding club houses,

incredible residential offering, premier golf courses,

welcoming staff, world-class culinary offerings and our

custome Outdoor Pursuit programs; all offering the

highest of service standards to enrich your experiance.

The Discovery Story

For almost three decades, Discovery has built communities that encourage both

our development teams and members themselves to dream up the most fun

experiences imaginable. The first development in Scottsdale set the standard for a

Discovery community, complete with a championship golf course,

stocked-to-the-brim comfort stations, luxurious residential offerings, five-star

service, and more; since then, each community has built on the fun from the one

that came before.
